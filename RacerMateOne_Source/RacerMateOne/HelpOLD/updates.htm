Updates to RacerMate One. 

Over time there will be updates and fixes applied to RacerMate One.  When these updates arise you will need to go to our update page and download these updates and apply them to your current version of RacerMate One.  Because a very large number of users do not have their computers connected to the Internet at all time, or at all, we were not willing to make this an automated update routine.  Instead it is simply going to be a download you must accomplish.

If there is an update you will find it here:

www.computrainer.com/rm1/updates.htm

What do I do if I don't have Internet?

The best and easiest way to do updates to RacerMate One is to use a pocket USB drive.  These come in many sizes, shapes, and colors and cost very little.  They are perfect for transferring files from a computer with Internet to a computer without the Internet.  They will appear as a new hard drive on your computer, drivers are automatically installed for them, and when you are done with the updates you pull them out and put them into your pocket.

If you have trouble getting or installing updates - please feel free to contact us anytime by emailing us.
